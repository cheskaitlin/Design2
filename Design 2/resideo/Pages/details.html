Overview

Excellent Pacific Heights Location. 17-unit multi-family building located in the heart of Pacific Heights, one of the finest neighborhoods in San Francisco. Constructed in 1895, the building consists of 17 efficiency units boasting natural light and beautiful architectural details throughout. Many of the units have been renovated, including new bathrooms, hardwood floors, cabinets, sinks and paint. 12 storage spaces, and coin operated laundry facility. All units have separate electric meters and heat.
